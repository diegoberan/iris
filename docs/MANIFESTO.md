# The Embodied AI Manifesto

Assistants live inside apps. Close the tab and they cease to exist.
Your devices — full of sensors, speakers, and GPUs — are reduced to chat windows.

## We believe

1. **A personal AI should persist.** A brain, not a session.
2. **Devices are bodies, not clients.** A body doesn't need arms — a phone has eyes,
   ears, and a voice.
3. **Devices should expose capabilities, not applications.**
4. **Intelligence should run wherever it is closest and cheapest** — your GPU, any cloud,
   any model.
5. **The user owns the brain** — its memory, its data, its infrastructure.

## What follows

A protocol, not a platform: bodies announce what they can do;
one brain decides where each action runs.

---

**One Brain. Multiple Bodies.**
