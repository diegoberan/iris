Our beliefs

Why Iris exists.

01

Persistent, not session-based

Your AI doesn't reset when you close a tab. It's a brain, always on.

02

Bodies, not clients

Every device is an extension of the same agent — eyes, ears, a voice, not a login screen.

03

Capabilities, not apps

Devices announce what they can do. The brain decides where each action runs.

04

Compute goes where it's cheapest

Your GPU first, the cloud when needed — routed live, any model.

05

You own the brain

Its memory, its data, its infrastructure. Not ours.
